Our Story

Thanh was born from the love of fresh sustainable seafood, and the zest of Asian cuisine. We at Thanh wanted to the bring the two things that we love, and share them with the world! After searching for the perfect location, we settled into a quaint spot in the San Francisco Bay area that we now call home. With us we bought new recipes, twist on old recipes, classics and fresh sustainable seafood delivered daily. We invite you to celebrate togetherness, life, and good food with our family.

Written by Onassis L. 2018
